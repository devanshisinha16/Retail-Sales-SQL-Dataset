# Retail-Sales-SQL-Dataset
This dataset contains three tables: customers products orders. These can be used to practice joins, aggregations, filtering, and sales analytics.

The dataset is used to answer the following sales analytics questions through SQL:

🟢 Easy
How many total customers are in the dataset?
How many orders were placed?
Which cities have the most customers?
What are the top 5 most purchased products?

🟡 Medium
What is the total revenue generated?
Which product category generates the most revenue?
What is the average order quantity?
Which cities generate the most revenue?

🔴 Hard (for beginners)
Who are the top 10 highest spending customers?
Which month has the highest sales?
What is the average spending per customer?
Which product category sells the most units?
What is the most common payment method?
